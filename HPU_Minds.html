WHO ARE WE?

HPU Minds

HPU Minds is a team of High Point University computer science and interdisciplinary students who are working with Steve Wozniak (High Point University's Innovator In Residence) to design and create exciting new projects. So far projects have included aspects such as computer vision, artificial Intelligence, electrical and mechanical engineering

THIS YEAR'S GOAL

This year, The Woz Project is at the forefront of innovation, leveraging cutting-edge technology to make a meaningful impact in the field of mental health. Our current initiative focuses on using Unreal Engine and C++ to develop immersive Virtual Reality (VR) simulations designed to aid exposure therapy for individuals with arachnophobia.
